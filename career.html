Two careers that I'm interested in are Software Developers and Hardware Engineers.


Career Description Salary Experience/Education Required
Software Developers
  • Research, design, and develop: computer, network software, and/or specialized utility programs.
  • Update software or enhance existing software capabilities.
  • Maintain databases within an application area.
$51.69 per hour, $107,510 per year 72% recieve at least a bachelors degree, 26% recieve at least a masters, and 3% recieve a doctorate.
Hardware Engineers
  • Research, design, develop, and/or test computer or computer-related equipment.
  • Supervise the manufacturing and installation of computer or computer-related equipment and components.
$56.36 per hour, $117,220 per year 99% recieve at least a bachelors degree, 32% recieve at least a masters, and 5% recieve a doctorate.
